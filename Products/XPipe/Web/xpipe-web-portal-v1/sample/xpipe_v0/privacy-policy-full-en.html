XPipe Infrastructure

XPipe is a PaaS platform developed by Sindria using Magento 2 and custom backend microservices. It runs on a high-availability, bare-metal Kubernetes infrastructure.

Security and authentication

XPipe uses Keycloak for SSO login. Sessions are secured via cryptographically signed tokens transmitted over HTTPS.

Preference management

Access control lists (ACLs) are stored client-side in the session. Users can manage their preferences from within the authenticated dashboard.
